[#] INTRODUCTION

CodePVG Leaderboard is a collaborative LeetCode tracking platform built specifically for PVG students.

[?] HOW_IT_WORKS

We automatically track your daily algorithmic progress and celebrate your problem-solving streaks. Whether you're grinding for technical interviews or just cracking logic puzzles for fun, every problem you solve automatically syncs and pushes you higher up the global ranks.

[~] Daily Auto-Sync

  • > Leaderboard refreshes daily at 12:05 AM UTC
  • > Tracks your daily, weekly, monthly, and overall stats
  • > Log on every morning to see updated rankings

[+] Score Multipliers

  • > Easy = 1 point
  • > Medium = 3 points
  • > Hard = 5 points

[>] JOINING_THE_GRID

Simply hit the Register button and link your exact LeetCode Username. Once verified, the system will fetch your stats and inject you into the main database!
